Vertical Rhythm

Oh, hey there! Here’s a look at some paragraph text. It’s very important that our text follows a vertical rhythm in order to keep our readers engaged.

We’re using an 8px grid, with a 16px base font size with 24px line height. Everything should line up perfectly to our 8px grid and be extremely pleasing to the eye. Yay maths!
